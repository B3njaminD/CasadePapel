Season 2
The biggest robbery in history is threatened. The Professor's plans are mistreated when the police discover the hideout where he orchestrated the junkyard with his team. Nothing is going right between Berlin, Tokyo, Denver and the rest of the group, where internal quarrels threaten to separate them. For his part, Raquel continues his investigation, tightening the noose around the true identity of the operation's brain. Will the Professor and his team manage to get out of the bank with all the money without losing anyone?
Season 3
After fleeing the National Mint and Stamp Factory, the Professor receives a call: one of the members of the gang has been captured! The only way to help him while protecting others is to bring them together to make a new robbery, the biggest breakage ever imagined. But again, the plan will not go as planned: this time it is the plan of the professor and not that of his father. Lots of twists and turns in this season 3: will characters end up in the hands of the police? "It is no longer a theft but to destroy the system: We are at War". Here are the Professor's last words at the end of the season, which is enough to announce a season 4 full of action.
